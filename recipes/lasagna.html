Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked Italian pasta dish made of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with fillings and baked. The pasta can be ruffled or have ridges to help trap sauce and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheet
  • Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce
  3. Combine Cheese Mixture
  4. Layer and Bake
